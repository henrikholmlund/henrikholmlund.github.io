what's this? Drumming you say?

I'm not only a drummer but also a recordist with a long experience of getting the right sounds for diverse styles. Loops are nice for sure, but if you want a human touch it's way easier and faster to let someone record drums for your projects

Send me your files in wav format and BPM for easy reference. Also along with any ideas of how you would like your project to sound like. References are welcome! I'll send you back the files in whichever format you want.
